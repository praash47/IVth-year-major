HTML Style Guide (Ryan Sechrest)
https://gist.github.com/ryansechrest/8693303

Documenting HTML:
We just specify sections in relevant places like
<!-- Profile -->
<!-- Header -->

We add closing tags as such to tags that donot have descriptive names like div, span,
section, article etc.
For eg: <div class="job-description">
We keep after the closing tag of this with </div>  <!-- ./job-description -->
*****
<div class="job-description">
blah blah blah
  <div class="job-desc-part">
  more blah
  </div>  <!-- ./job-desc-part -->
</div>  <!-- ./job-description -->
*****
Notice the two spaces after closing tag.

for id: <!-- #/job-description -->
